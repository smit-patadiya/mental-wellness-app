# MindMitra — Market Scan & Differentiation

> What existing wellness apps do, and where MindMitra is genuinely different. No code.

## The landscape
| App | What it does | Gap for our user |
|-----|--------------|------------------|
| **Wysa** (India, Touchkin) | CBT+DBT + meditation, penguin mascot, mood tracking, journaling, crisis→helplines, optional human coaches | General wellness; not exam-specific; retention-oriented chat |
| **Youper** (psychiatrist-founded) | CBT daily check-ins, mood graphs, adaptive ACT/mindfulness exercises | General; onboarding + daily-engagement model |
| **Woebot** | CBT conversational, RCT-backed | Shut its consumer app; CBT-chat only |
| **Calm / Headspace** | meditation, sleep stories, audio | Not personalized, no analysis; **built to maximize time-in-app** |
| **Amaha (InnerHour) / MindPeers / MannSetu** (India) | self-help tools + therapy, "make it cool" | Broad; not laser-focused on exam stress |
| **CBSE "Dost for Life"** | govt student psychosocial app | Institutional, generic |

**Universal limitation (every source says it):** these apps *supplement*, never replace therapy/crisis care. We hold the same line — and route crises to humans (like Wysa does).

## Where MindMitra is different (our moat)
1. **Return-to-study, anti-engagement** ← the big one. Everyone else optimizes *retention* (more time in app = success). We optimize the **opposite**: steady the student in ≤2 min and send them **back to studying**. No streaks, no infinite feed, no retention hooks. For a student who can't afford lost study time, this is the right *and* unique design.
2. **Exam-laser + India** — purpose-built for NEET/JEE/CUET/CAT/GATE/UPSC pressure, Indian helplines, exam timeline. Others are general wellness.
3. **Hidden-pattern detection from open-ended text** — others show mood graphs; we surface *multi-factor triggers across time* ("dips after evening mocks + low sleep") — the brief's headline ask.
4. **One-input, friction-zero** — tap/talk/type once; AI infers the rest. Others are onboarding- and chat-heavy.
5. **Local-first privacy** — journals never leave the device. Most competitors are cloud accounts.
6. **Calm-minimal, no mascot** — quiet and serious-but-warm, not a cartoon or "cool" branding.

## One-line positioning
*"Every wellness app wants you to stay. MindMitra wants you calm and back to your books in two minutes."*
