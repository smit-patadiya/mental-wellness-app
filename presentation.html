The problem

India's students are in a silent crisis

69.9%

report moderate–high anxiety (2025, 8 cities)

14,488

student suicides in 2024 (NCRB) — exam failure the top cause for under-18s

<10%

of youth ever access any mental-health help (UNICEF)

They face severe stress, burnout, and self-doubt — and bottle it up. Standard mood-slider apps can't see why.

The idea

Read what students actually feel — not just a 1–5 slider

Standard trackers

"Rate your mood: 😀😐😟". A number. No context. Misses the real triggers.

MindMitra

Student just talks or types one line. Gemini reads the open-ended text and uncovers the hidden trigger a slider can't — "mood dips after evening mocks + low sleep."
